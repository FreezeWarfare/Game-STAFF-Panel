Last Updated 22/06/2019

As always, staff have the final say on reports, situations and disagreements. Equally, the rules might change from time to time, so check back regularly to keep up to date.

GENERAL
1.1 - No racism, sexism, homophobia etc etc. We live in a multicultural, multi belief world, and welcome everyone here to our community.
1.2 - Respect your fellow members. We won’t always agree on everything, so let’s keep things respectful.
1.3 - Bullying in any form is not allowed.
1.4 - NSFW. This isn’t a meme dumping ground for questionable material. If you have to consider whether it’s appropriate, don’t post it!
1.5 - We welcome discussions about games and communities, however being here with the sole purpose of recruiting is not allowed.
1.6 - Do not impersonate a staff member. Help is always welcomed but you should not pretend to be staff.
1.7 - Do not chase staff for decisions / requests. They will be looked at and dealt with when time can be assigned to it.
1.8 - Stream sniping is forbidden. Show respect to our content creators.
1.9 - Our servers are microphone only. You must have a working microphone to play here.
GAME
2.1 - No Racism, sexism, rape, pedophilia or other such acts.
2.2 - Hacking, exploiting or otherwise manipulating the game to your advantage is not allowed.
2.3 - Trolling, ear rape, powergaming, metagaming or other such behaviour is not allowed.
2.4 - No RDM (random deathmatch) - You're not permitted to shoot at someone without having roleplayed with clear consequences.
- Drive-by's are acceptable if within a roleplay scenario. Abuse of this privilege will result in it being removed.
2.5 - Combat logging of any description is not allowed.
2.6 - Military vehicles are not allowed.
2.7 - Vehicles with mounted weapons are not allowed.
2.8 - You're not allowed to use weapons found on locals (AI).
2.9 - Fail RP can fall under many groups. Here are some examples;
- Selecting invisible items from the character creation menu or clothes shop.
- Not valuing your life.
- Deliberately incapacitating yourself to avoid arrest.
- Using emotes to get through walls or doors.
- Dropping items whilst handcuffed.
- Failing to roleplay with LSPD or EMS
- Not complying with EMS requests.
- Forcing people to take money out of ATM's.
- Going OOC (Out Of Character).
- Robbing stores from outside the respective building.
These are just a few examples and is by no means an extensive list. So, if in doubt, speak with a member of staff who will be happy to bring you up to speed with the standards we expect.
2.10 - You're to roleplay with everyone.....yes, even locals!
2.11 - Going AFK for anything longer than 10 minutes is not allowed. If you need to go AFK for long periods of time, you are to log out.
2.12 - A maximum of 5 people are permitted to act as a group (if not in a Club activity). For example; robbing banks, jewellery stores or other illegal activities.
2.13 - You're allowed four roleplay characters on the server. This extends to the LSPD and EMS. Each character must be kept separate from your others and each have their own backstory. So yes, you can be a civilian, cop and paramedic! Do not abuse this system.
2.14 - Crime is not permitted within the first and last 15 minutes of server restarts. This is to allow people time to settle in and wind down.
2.15 - You're not allowed to steal EMS or FIB vehicles.
2.16 - You're not allowed to use untextured or invisible clothing options.
2.17 - /ooc is only to be used if you're having techical issues, not a place to throw salt around.
POLICE & EMS
3.1 - Corruption within Government Departments is not allowed.
3.2 - Only LSPD and EMS are permitted to use aircraft.
3.3 - Civilians are not allowed to dress like LSPD or EMS.
3.4 - You're not allowed to troll the 911 call system.
3.5 - When in the holding cell area of Mission Row, you're to be on whisper at all times.
3.6 - Civilians are not permitted to use the K9 system.
3.7 - If you have been sent to jail, you're not permitted to switch characters until your jail time has been completed.
3.8 - You're not allowed to kill another inmate at Boilingbroke Prison.
3.9 - Hostages must be genuine.
3.10 - There needs to be a minimum of 4 Police on duty for hostages to be taken.
3.11 - Making unrealistic demands for the sake of a gunfight is not allowed.
3.12 - You are to remain in medical custody until the EMS representative releases you. Failing to abide by this rule is considered FailRP.
3.13 - You're not allowed to rob, threaten, kidnap etc an EMS representative.
3.14 - If you have been incompacitated you're only allowed to communicate it the /me command (this means no voice).
3.15 - We have basic New Life Rules;
- IF YOU HAVE BEEN REVIVED -
You may remember all events up to being incompacitated but may not remember anything whilst on the ground.
- IF YOU HAVE RESPAWNED -
You must stay away from the place you died for 15 minutes. You are to not remember the events leading to your death.
3.16 - We have a couple of green zones dotted around where no crime is permitted. These are within the grounds of;
- Hospitals
- Fire Stations
- Garages
CLUBS
4.1 - Clubs require a thorough backstory posted on the forums in the appropriate section. All of their actions must be justifiable by that story.
4.2 - Club stories need to be approved by an Admin or above.
4.3 - Clubs are limited to a maximum of 5 players in game committing criminal activities at the same time (a maximum of 15 players in the club. Names and forum accounts must be specified in original club post).
4.4 - All club members must have a forum account with their Steam ID updated and displayed on their profile.
4.5 - When active as a club all members must roll together when committing crimes or conducting illegal (gang) activities (may not have multiple groups running around committing different crimes).
4.6 - Conflicts between clubs are permitted, however they must be clearly defined in regards to both clubs RP stories and announced on the forums.
4.7 - Clubs required to have a defining element to their clothing (for example, Bikers could have leather waistcoats), which needs to be outlined in the forum post.
4.8 - Clubs can only claim territory via roleplay and are limited to one base of operations.
4.9 - A player is only allowed to be part of 1 club.
4.10 - Clubs face removal if mutliple rule breaks are observed.
4.11 - To be eligible for TeamSpeak tags and rooms, clubs must have the following;
- 5 or more members.
- An approved backstory on the forums.
REPORTS
5.1 - Player reports are ONLY to be made on the forums. We do not offer a TeamSpeak resolution service.
5.2 - Reports are to be made within 48 hours of the incident happening and include 2 minutes of prior video (if applicable).
5.3 - Reports are to use the given template. Those that do not will be closed.
5.4 - Do not post on a report unless you're either invited to do so or were part of the incident.
5.5 - Decisions are final, you may only appeal if you're invited to do so.
5.6 - No ban evading. If you have been banned on one account, you're not allowed to use another account to join the server.
5.7 - Revenge reports are not allowed. Be an adult and accept when you've done wrong.
5.8 - You're not allowed to use evidence that is not your own with the persmission of the owner.
5.9 - When reviewing evidence, Staff will look at the entire situation which means your conduct will also be checked.
